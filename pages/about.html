Symposium
of Gods and Heroes 650BC

The Heroes Symposium is an event that aims to reune all the great heroes of mythology for an day full of events. Almost all of the speakers where of greek decent, but this year Gilgamesh the King of Uruk and the oldest hero in recorded history has joined the event to share his tales of glory. So be prepared for a jolly time with all our programs that where designed for you to have a full experience being close to such legends.
